Researching & Teaching London

There are more than 280 researchers at King’s, based in every faculty, who focus on London in their work. Drawing on this research, they teach over 25 different modules, in a wide variety of disciplines, examining London as a scholarly subject.

Here you'll find a forum for staff and PhD students researching and teaching about London at King’s. Includes a space to share teaching resources and details of relevant events within and beyond the College, and a message board to contact other members of the network.
